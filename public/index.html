How to Set a Search Alert

            
    
              
  1. 
                
    Go to Airbnb and run a search
    
                
    Visit Airbnb, log into your account, and perform a normal search for the destination and dates you are interested in.
    
                
              
    2. 
              
  2. 
                
    Apply your preferred filters
    
                
    On the search results page, apply the filters you want included in your alert (dates, price range, guests, property type, and amenities).
    
                
              
    3. 
              
  3. 
                
    Copy the search URL from Airbnb
    
                
    Once your filters are set, copy the full URL from your browser address bar.
    
                
              
    4. 
              
  4. 
                
    Paste the URL in Alert Buzz and save
    
                
    Paste the URL into the field above and click Save Alert. You’ll receive email notifications when matches are detected.
